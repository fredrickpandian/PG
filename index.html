Fredrick Vasanth Pandian

Visionary. Leader. Problem Solver.

Fredrick Vasanth Pandian is the Founder of Pandian Global and a global executive, finance, operations, and technology leader with more than 25 years of experience across the U.S. and Asia.

His work focuses on helping organizations modernize systems, strengthen operations, improve financial visibility, and use technology, automation, and AI to work smarter.

Fredrick brings deep nonprofit management expertise, including executive leadership, finance administration, budgeting, governance support, internal systems, and organizational transformation.

Born in Tamil Nadu and immigrating to the United States at age 14, Fredrick brings a global perspective, practical problem-solving mindset, and deep commitment to building systems that help people and organizations thrive.
